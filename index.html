(G)-IDLE

(G)-IDLE is a five member South Korean Girl Group formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018. The group consists of: Miyeon, Minnie, Soyeon, Yuqi, and Shuhua. Unlike most K-pop girl groups, (G)I-dle are directly involved in the creation of their music; in particular, Soyeon who has written and produced the majority of the group's songs. Members Minnie and Yuqi have also co-written and produced numerous album tracks.
